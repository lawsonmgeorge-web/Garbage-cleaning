Hi, I'm Lawson George

I recently turned 16 and have always enjoyed learning new skills and trades. This summer, I decided to start a garbage can cleaning business to provide a useful service to my community while gaining real-world business experience.

My goal is to eventually get into automotive detailing, but I wanted to start with a service that helps people keep their homes cleaner and fresher. Running this business is helping me learn customer service, responsibility, and the value of hard work.

Every job is completed with care, and I take pride in doing quality work at an affordable price. Whether you need a one-time cleaning or regular service, I'd be happy to help.

Thank you for supporting a local young entrepreneur. If you'd like to schedule a cleaning, feel free to contact me anytime.
